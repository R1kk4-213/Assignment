Program in Action

Tutor Support Program at HCMUT

A structured academic mentoring program connecting HCMUT students with qualified tutors.

Overview

The Tutor Support Program helps students overcome learning challenges, improve academic performance, and build confidence in key courses through targeted one-on-one and small-group mentoring. The program connects students, tutors, academic departments, and university offices via a centralized online platform that handles registration, matching, scheduling, session tracking, and reporting.

Program Objectives
  • Academic improvement for students: better exam preparation, clearer study plans, and stronger fundamentals.
  • Professional growth for tutors: practice communication, teaching, and leadership with recognized mentoring experience.
  • Transparency and data for the university: usage analytics, engagement reports, and insights for scholarships, quality assurance, and program evaluation.
Who Can Join
  • Students (Mentees): any HCMUT student can search and filter tutor profiles, request mentoring, book sessions, and track their learning progress.
  • Tutors (Mentors): faculty members, graduate students, and high-performing senior students approved by departments.
  • Program Coordinators & Departments: approve tutor applicants, monitor session quality, and use reports to guide improvements.
How the Program Works
  1. Students sign in with their HCMUT SSO account and complete a short profile.
  2. They browse available tutors, filter by course, topic, language, and availability, then submit a tutoring request.
  3. Tutors review and accept requests, then agree on a schedule (online or on campus).
  4. After each session, both tutor and student record quick feedback; the system aggregates data for progress tracking and reporting.
Key System Features
1. Unified Login & Data Sync
  • Accounts linked with the university’s DATACORE / student information system.
  • Automatic import of course enrollment, helping recommend relevant tutors.
2. Smart Matching & Scheduling
  • AI-based suggestions recommend suitable tutors to each student.
  • Conflict-aware calendar with color-coded time slots.
3. Professional Session Management
  • Structured agendas and session notes.
  • Attendance tracking and learning goals per topic.
  • Downloadable session logs for scholarship or portfolio evidence.
4. Feedback & Reporting
  • Real-time dashboards for courses, semesters, and departments.
  • Trend lines showing improvement and high-risk topics.
  • Exportable summary reports for accreditation and quality assurance.
Benefits
For Students
  • Personalized academic support and better preparation for exams.
  • Higher GPA potential and reduced stress.
  • Clearer study plans and improved confidence.
For Tutors
  • Teaching experience recognized by HCMUT.
  • Opportunities to develop communication, mentoring, and leadership skills.
  • Stronger CV for jobs or graduate studies.
For the University
  • Centralized overview of tutoring activities across faculties.
  • Data-driven decisions on course support and curriculum design.
  • Improved student success and retention.
Vision for the Future

The Tutor Support Program is designed as the foundation of a modern academic support ecosystem at HCMUT. Future extensions include integration with LMS platforms, learning analytics dashboards, and additional mentoring services such as career advising and soft-skills training.
